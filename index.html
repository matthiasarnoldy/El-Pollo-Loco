Tutorial

Goal: Kill or avoid the chickens and defeat the boss chicken!

Control:

  • A / Arrow left - Move left
  • D / Arrow right - Move right
  • Space - Jump
  • F / Enter - Throw salsa bottles

Extras: Collect coins and salsa bottles!

Story: During his journey through Mexico, Pepe el Peligroso passes by a cockfight and suddenly finds himself in his own battle against dangerous chickens. They even have a ruthless leader, El Pollo Brutal, and he is not to be messed with. Stay sharp and help Pepe survive this fight.
